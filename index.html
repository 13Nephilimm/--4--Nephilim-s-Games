Roll The Dice!

It's a minigame for 2 players. You need to roll the dice. If you your rolled dice is 2, 3, 4, 5 or 6 you can hold your score and then will be your friend's turn. Also you can continue rolling but keep in mind that if your rolled dice will be 1, you will lose all of your points and then will be your friend's turn. First player who gets 100 points wins. Good luck! ohh and main rule: HAVE FUN!!!

BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The formula is BMI = kg/m2 where kg is a person's weight in kilograms and m2 is their height in metres squared. A BMI of 25.0 or more is overweight, while the healthy range is 18.5 to 24.9.
